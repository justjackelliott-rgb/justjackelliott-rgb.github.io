Jack Elliott is a sophomore and is pursuing a B.S. in Broadcast & Digital Journalism from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. He currently calls games for the Syracuse Club Baseball Team and for Syracuse Esports. He also works for WAER Sports.

When he isn't broadcasting, Jack sings with his A Cappella group Otto Tunes. This year, they will be competing in the International Competition for Collegiate A Cappella on January 31st.
